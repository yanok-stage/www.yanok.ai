Bold Commerce Integrations

Bold Commerce provides a selection of apps that help convert more customers, maximize average order value and increase customer lifetime value. Specifically the Bold Subscriptions app allows merchants to build a custom recurring revenue solution.

New Subscription
Triggers when a customer checks out with a new subscription.
New Subscription Order
Triggers when a new subscription order gets generated.
Subscription Cancelled
Triggers when a subscription gets cancelled.
