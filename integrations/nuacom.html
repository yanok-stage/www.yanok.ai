NUACOM Integrations

NUACOM is a unified communication platform and phone system to automate your call process.

Triggers

New Incoming Call
Triggers when a new incoming call is received (includes answered and missed calls).
New Incoming Missed Call
Triggers when a new incoming call is missed.
New Call
Triggers when there is a new call (includes incoming, outgoing for answered and missed).
New Outgoing Answered Call
Triggers when a new outgoing answered call is made.
New Outgoing Call
Triggers when a new outgoing call is made (includes answered and unanswered calls).

Actions

Find Contact
Find a contact in NUACOM phone book.
