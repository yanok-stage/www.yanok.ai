DreamCampaigns Integrations

DreamCampaigns is an email & SMS marketing platform. Create beautiful and engaging emails & texts and send them to your subscribers.

Triggers

New Email Bounce
Triggers when a campaign email to a Subscriber bounces (ie. could not be delivered).
New Bounced Transactional
Triggers when a transactional email bounces.
New Client
Triggers when a client is added to your account in DreamCampaigns (Account level API keys required).
New Email Campaign Starts Sending
Triggers when a new email campaign starts sending.
New Click Through
Triggers when someone clicks through an email from one of your Campaigns.
New Email Open
Triggers when someone opens an email from one of your Email Campaigns.
New Form Submit
Triggers when a form has been submitted.
New List
Triggers when a list is added for a client.
New SMS Campaign Starts Sending
Triggers when a new SMS campaign starts sending.
New Subscriber
Triggers when a new subscriber is added to a list. The subscriber may be an email or a mobile subscriber.
New Unsubscribe
Triggers when a someone unsubscribes from a list. The unsubscribe may be an email or a mobile subscriber.

Actions

Create Email Campaign
Creates and sends a new email campaign.
Track Revenue
Adds new Revenue to your Revenue Target
Create SMS Campaign
Creates and sends a new SMS campaign.
SMS Unsubscribe
Remove a SMS subscriber from a list.
Add/Update Subscriber
Create a new subscriber on a list or update a current subscriber.
Send Smart Transactional Email
Sends a smart transactional email.
Send Smart Transactional SMS
Sends a smart transactional sms.
Email Unsubscribe
Remove an email subscriber from a list.
Re-Subscribe a SMS Subscriber
Update a sms subscriber status.
Re-Subscribe an Email Subscriber
Update an email subscriber status.
Find Subscriber
Find a subscriber by email or mobile within a given list.
