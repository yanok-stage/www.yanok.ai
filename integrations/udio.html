Udio Integrations

Udio is a booking management software designed to help business owners and managers streamline their day-to-day operations.

Triggers

Billing Account Updated
Triggers when Billing Account is updated.
Event Triggered
Triggers when any Event is happening.
Achievement Completed
Triggers when Achievement has been completed.
Unable to Attend
Triggers when Single Booking on particular date and time, has been Cancelled or customer used 'Unable to attend' form.
First Booking in Course
Triggers when Customer was booked into the Course for the first time.
First Booking in Facility
Triggers when Customer was booked in the facility first time.
New Free Booking
Triggers when New booking was made in free single bookings Course (e.g. Assessment).
Voucher Booking Moved
Triggers when Voucher Bookings have been Moved: date, time or course have been changed.
New Voucher Booking
Triggers when New Booking has been made with voucher (e.g. Make Up or Free Trial).
Course Completed
Triggers when Course has been completed.
New Customer
Triggers when New family has been added or customer has signed up.
Online Signup of Existing Customer
Triggers when Existing customer created customer accounts login.
Email Address Confirmation
Triggers when Email address confirmation during initial online signup, or while adding email.
Invoice Cancelled
Triggers when Invoice has been cancelled.
New Invoice
Triggers when Invoice has been created.
Invoice Outstanding (1 Day)
Triggers when Invoice was raised 1 day ago and is still unpaid.
Invoice Outstanding (14 Day)
Triggers when Invoice was raised two weeks ago and is still unpaid.
Invoice Outstanding (3 Days)
Triggers when Invoice was raised 3 days ago and is still unpaid.
Invoice Outstanding (30 Days)
Triggers when Invoice was raised one month ago and is still unpaid.
Invoice Outstanding (7 Days)
Triggers when Invoice was raised a week ago and is still unpaid.
Invoice Fully Paid
Triggers when One invoice has been fully paid. Will be triggered multiple times per payment, for each invoice. Use 'Payment Successful' to send one message per payment.
Missed 2 Bookings
Triggers when Customer has missed 2 bookings in a row.
Missed 3 Bookings
Triggers when Customer has missed 3 bookings in a row.
Missed 4 Bookings
Triggers when Customer has missed 4 bookings in a row.
Missed 5 Bookings
Triggers when Customer has missed 5 bookings in a row.
Online Password Reset
Triggers when Password Reset Email for Customer Accounts.
Automatic Payment Failed
Triggers when Provider Payment has failed.
Payment Used Outstanding Credit
Triggers when Outstanding credit has been used to pay some invoices.
Payment Method Added
Triggers when Payment method was added.
Payment Method Removed
Triggers when Payment method was removed.
Payment Reversed
Triggers when Payment has been reversed.
Payment Successful
Triggers when Payment has succeeded or was taken over the counter.
Provisional Booking Timeout
Triggers when During online enrolment customer did not finish the booking process and it has timed out.
Regular Booking Has Been Cancelled
Triggers when Regular Booking (Purchase) has been Cancelled. No more attendance after particular date.
Regular Booking Moved
Triggers when Regular Booking (Purchase) has been Moved: date, time or Course have been updated.
New Regular Booking
Triggers when Regular Booking (Purchase) has been created or customer enrolled into a course.
Qualification Expiring
Triggers when Qualification expires in 30 days.
Customer Added
Triggers when Customer was placed on the waiting list.
Customer Deleted
Triggers when Customer was deleted from the waiting list.
Customer Invited
Triggers when Customer on waiting list has been invited.
