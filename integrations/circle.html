Circle Integrations

A modern community platform for creators.

Triggers

Member Removed
Triggers when a member is removed from the community.
New Space Created
Triggers when a new space is created.
New Comment Posted
Triggers when a new comment is posted.
New Community
Triggers when a new community is created by the user.
New Member Active Subscription
Triggers when a new member subscription is activated. This includes trial subscriptions.
New Member Canceled Subscription
Triggers when a member subscription is cancelled.
New Member Paid Charge
Triggers when a member charge is paid.
New Member Past Due Subscription
Triggers when a member subscription becomes past due.
New Member Refunded Charge
Triggers when a member charge is refunded.
New Event Created
Triggers when a new event is created.
New Event RSVP
Triggers when a member RSVPs into an event.
New Tagged Member
Triggers when a member is tagged with a member tag.
New Post
Triggers when a new post is created.
New Member Added
Triggers when a new member joins the community, or when a member is added by an admin with a password set.

Add Member to Space Group

Create Comment
Create a new comment.
Create Event
Create an event in your community
Create Post
Creates a new post on a community.
Invite Member
Invite a member to your community.
Remove Member From Space Group
Remove a member from a space group.
Remove Member From Space
Remove a member from a space.
Remove Member
Remove a member from your community.
RSVP Member to Event
RSVP a member into an event.
Send Direct Message
Send a direct message to a member.
Start a Group Chat
Start a group chat with multiple community members.
Tag Member
Tag a community member with a member tag.
Remove Member From Event
Remove a member's RSVP from an event
Untag Member
Remove a member tag from a community member.
Update Event
Update an existing event in the community
Update Member
Update community member
Find Member

Verify that a member has a specific member tag assigned to them on your community.

Verify that a member belongs to a specific space on your community.
