Call Assist Integrations

Call Assist connect your sales team with new leads instantly and follow up with aging leads automatically through the phone.

Triggers

Call Completed
Triggers when a call has been made to a Lead and the call completes.
Lead Updated or Tagged
Triggers when a lead has been updated.

Actions

Find Lead
Finds an existing Lead
Make Call to Team or Lead
Calls a Lead and plays your recorded message or a Team connecting the agent to the lead.
Scheduled Call to Team or Lead
Schedules a Call to a Team or Lead at a specified time.
Create or Update Lead
Creates a new lead if it does not exist. Updates a Lead if it exists.
