Apparound Integrations

Apparound is an app to digitise sales and improve sales productivity. Combines content and quoting together.

Triggers

New Quote
Triggers when a new quote is created (once you click next on the summary page).
Changed Quote Status
Triggers when the status of a quote is changed.

Actions

Create Customer
Create a new customer.
Create Product
Add a new product to the catalog.
Update Product Price
Update the pricing attributes of a product that belongs to a price list.
