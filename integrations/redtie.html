Redtie Integrations

Redtie is an advanced, feature rich text messaging solution that lets you send attachments just like you would in an email. Businesses can share any type of files over a simple text message to their customers or contacts over a text message.

Triggers

Business List
To list the Business details of the user based on their email address.
User List
Get the User Id based on the user.

Actions

Add Contact
Add contacts for your business.
Send Message
Sends message to a particular contact in your Redtie business.
