Curb Hero Integrations

Curb Hero (formerly Block Party) is a simple, yet powerful, digital Open House sign-in app + property marketing tool used by thousands of real estate agents and mortgage professionals. Their logo, photos, color palette, lead questions, and follow-up messaging are all customizable. Curb Hero's Zapier integration can be used to unlock infinite possibilities with the data collected at open houses or in property marketing!
