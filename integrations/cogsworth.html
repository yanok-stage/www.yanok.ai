Cogsworth Integrations

Cogsworth is an appointment and meeting scheduling app that allows people to book in time with you and your business when you are available. Cogsworth syncs with your calendar and pulls out your availability and also pushes your bookings back to your calendar.

Triggers

New Appointment
Triggers when a new appointment is booked.
Cancelled Appointment
Triggers when an appointment is cancelled.
New Appointment Request
Triggers when a new appointment is requested and is pending approval.
Appointment Rescheduled
Triggers when an appointment is rescheduled.
