Gmelius Integrations

Gmelius is a team collaboration platform to share emails, manage projects, and automate daily workflows right from Gmail and your other favorite apps.

Triggers

Updated Card
Triggers when a card is updated within a Kanban board in Gmail.
Updated Conversation
Triggers when email assignee, status, or tag is added or changed.
New Card
Triggers when a new card is created within a Kanban board in Gmail.
New Conversation
Triggers when a new conversation is received within a Gmelius workspace.
New Reply to Conversation
Triggers when a new email appears in a thread within a Gmelius workspace.
New Note
Triggers when a new note is added to an email or a task card.
New Sequence Enrollment
Triggers when a contact is added to a sequence in Gmelius.
New Sequence Unenrollment
Triggers when a contact is removed from a sequence in Gmelius.

Actions

Create Card
Creates a new task card within a Kanban board in Gmail.
Enroll in Sequence
Adds a contact in a sequence in Gmelius.
Unenroll From Sequence
Removes a contact from a specific sequence in Gmelius.
Update Card
Updates a task card : add tag, change status, assign, change due date.
Update Conversation
Updates a conversation : add tag, change status, assign.
