PhoneBurner Integrations

PhoneBurner is an outbound sales dialing platform that increases live client interactions and overall team productivity and transparency.

Triggers

Actions

Create Contact
Creates a new contact.
Update Contact (Contact ID)
This Action will update a specific contact within PhoneBurner based on the unique PhoneBurner contact/record ID. In order to use this Action, you must have the PhoneBurner contact ID. This will update the specific contact within your personal account or if you are the PhoneBurner admin it will update the contact if it is in your account or any one of your users accounts
