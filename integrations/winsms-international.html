WinSMS International Integrations

WinSMS is a B2B bulk SMS messaging gateway which makes it quick and easy for businesses wishing to integrate SMS messaging into their workflow and business processes.

Triggers

New Reply
Triggers when a new reply SMS is received. Replies from US numbers are currently not supported by this trigger. Unicode or emoji characters in replies may not appear as is in the response.

Actions

Send SMS
Sends an SMS message to the specified recipient(s).
