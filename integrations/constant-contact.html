Constant Contact Integrations

Constant Contact can help grow your business with an email marketing tool that’s affordable, powerful, and easy to use.

Triggers

New Contact
Triggers when a new contact is added to your account.
New Email Open
Triggers when a recipient opens an email from a specified campaign activity.
New List
Triggers when a new list is added.

Actions

Create Contact
Creates a contact on a specific list in your account.
Update Contact
Updates a contact in your account. Empty values will be cleared on the contact. If the contact being updated is deleted, the contact will be revived.
Find or Create Contact
Find Contact by email or create a new one.
