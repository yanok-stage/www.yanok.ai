Urable Integrations

Urable is an application designed for automotive businesses that specialize in detailing, paint protection film, vinyl wrapping, tinting, and ceramic coating. As a cloud-based application with dedicated mobile apps, it allows you to run your business from multiple devices in real-time.

Triggers

New Customer
Triggers when a new customer is added in Urable.
New or Updated Customer
Triggers when a customer is created or updated in Urable.
New Event
Triggers when a new event is created.
New Job
Triggers when a new job is created.
New or Updated Event
Triggers when an event is created or updated.
New or Updated Job
Triggers when a job is created or updated.

Actions

Update/Create Customer
Finds a customer in Urable by email address. If the customer exists, they are updated. If they do not exist, they are created.
