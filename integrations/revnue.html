Revnue Integrations

Revnue is a digital contract management platform that provides insights across all your contracts, suppliers, customers, assets, and services.

Triggers

New Asset
Triggers when a new asset is created in your account.
New Contract
Triggers when a new contract is created in your account.
New Customer
Triggers when a new customer is created in your account.
New Supplier
Triggers when a new supplier is created in your account.

Actions

Create Asset
Creates an asset on your account.
Create Contract
Creates a contract on your account.
Create Customer
Creates a customer on your account.
Create Supplier
Creates a supplier on your account.
Update Asset
Updates an asset on your account.
Update Contract
Updates a contract on your account.
Update Customer
Updates a customer on your account.
Update Supplier
Updates a supplier on your account.
Upload File
Uploads a file from a cloud drive (Google Drive, DropBox, OneDrive, etc.) directly to your Revnue Smart Repository.
