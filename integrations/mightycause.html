Mightycause is a fundraising software for the modern nonprofit.

Triggers

New Brand Donation
Triggers when a donation is made to a brand you manage on Mightycause.
New Donation
Triggers when a donation is made to your fundraiser or organization.
New Fundraiser Interaction
Triggers when someone shares, likes or comments on your fundraiser or organization.
New Fundraiser/Team
Triggers when someone creates a charitable fundraiser or team for your organization.
Giving Event Registration Created or Updated
Triggers when a Giving Event Registration is created or has its approval status updated.
Organization Administrator Created or Updated
Receives data on organization administrators whenever a new administrator is created or updated
