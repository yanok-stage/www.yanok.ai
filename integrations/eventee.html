Eventee Integrations

Eventee is an intuitive mobile and web app that enhances attendee experience and engagement at in-person, virtual or hybrid events.

Triggers

New Attendee
Triggers when an imported or invited attendee joins your event in Eventee.
Attendee Invitation
Triggers when an attendee is imported or invited to your event in Eventee.
New Visitor
Triggers when a user visits your event in the Eventee app.

Actions

Invite Attendee
Invites a new attendee to your event in Eventee.
