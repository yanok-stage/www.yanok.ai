Revaluate Integrations

Revaluate is an artificial intelligence platform for the real estate and mortgage industries that helps agents and loan officers know when they contacts are likely to move.

Triggers

Contact Ascend 80
Triggers when a contact's Revaluate Score goes from below 80 to above 80 making them likely to move.
Contact Descend 80
Triggers when a contact whose score was above 80, drops below 80.
Contact Weekly Score Trigger
Triggers weekly to allow for updating of all contact scores in an external system.
