Cocosign Integrations

CocoSign is an easy, affordable alternative to DocuSign, helping SMBs and professionals streamline eSignature workflows immensely and sign contracts 5x faster.

Triggers

Document Completed
Triggers when all signing parties have signed the document.
Document Sent
Triggers when you send out a new signature request.
Document Declined
Triggers when any signing party declines to sign your document.
Document Expired
Triggers when the document is expired.
Access Blocked
Triggers when signers were denied access to the documents because they entered the wrong authentication code three times in a row.
New Signature Request
Triggers when you receive a document to fill out and sign.
Document Viewed
Triggers when your document is viewed by your signer.
Document Voided
Triggers when the document is voided.

Actions

Create a Document From Templates
Create a new document from your pre-created templates and send it out for signatures.
