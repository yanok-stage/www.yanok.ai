Hello Audio Integrations

HelloAudio.fm is the audio host for digital marketers and course creators to reach their audience where they’re already listening.

Triggers

Listener Activates a Feed
This trigger fires when a listener activates their subscription to a feed.
Listener Downloads Specific Episode
Triggers when a listener downloads a specific episode from a specific feed.

Actions

Add Listener
Adds a listener in HelloAudio.
Edit Listener
Edits a listener in Hello Audio.
Add/Edit Listener Metadata
Add or update for a listener, some rich metadata that exists outside of a specific feed and is Hello-Audio wide.
