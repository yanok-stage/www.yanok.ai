Plivo Integrations

Plivo is an easy-to-use tool that lets you send & receive SMS and voice calls.

Triggers

New Completed Call
Triggers after a new call is completed.
New SMS
Triggers when you receive a new SMS. This will override the message_url associated with your Plivo number if you have already set it.

Actions

Make Call & Play Audio
Make a call and play an audio.
Make Call & Speak Text
Make a call and say a message.
Send MMS
Sends an multimedia message
Send SMS
Sends an SMS.
Find Number
Finds a phone number by service type.
