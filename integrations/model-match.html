Model Match Integrations

Model Match helps forward thinking mortgage lenders streamline the recruiting process and bring their team and pipeline into one central, collaborative space.

Triggers

Contact Is Added to a Requisition
Triggers when a contact is added to a requisition.
Contact Is Assigned
Triggers when a contact is assigned to a user.
Contact Is Marked as Touched
Triggers when a contact is marked as touched.
Contact Responded to Sequence
Triggers when a contact responds to a sequence.
Contact Stage Is Updated
Triggers when the contact stage has been updated.
Contact Updated
Triggers when a contact is updated.
Requisition Contact Is Tagged
Triggers when a requisition contact has been tagged.
To-Do Completed
Triggers when a To-Do is marked as complete.

Actions

Add a Contact
Adds a new user contact
Add Contact to a Sequence
Creates a new contact and adds it to a sequence
Add Contact to Requisition
Creates a new contact and adds it to a specified requisition
Assign a Contact to a User
Creates a new contact and adds it to a specified requisition. Then assigns the contact to a user
Update Contact Stage
Updates a Contact to a stage
