Submittable Integrations

Submittable brings your team together so you can collect, collaborate, and manage any application and review process, exactly the way you want.

Triggers

Fund Awarded
Triggers when a submission is awarded money from a fund.
Fund Paid
Triggers when awarded money is marked as paid in a submission.
Internal Form Updated
Triggers when a new internal form is submitted.
New Note
Triggers when a note is added to a submission.
New Team Member
Triggers when a new team member is added to your Submittable organization, if the new team member does not already have an account, this event triggers when the invitation is accepted.
New Submission
Triggers when new submissions arrive in your Submittable Account.
Reference Form Received
Triggers when a reference form is received for a submission.
Submission Status Changed
Triggers when a submission's status changes.
