KickoffLabs Integrations

KickoffLabs allows you to create giveaway campaigns in minutes!

Triggers

Lead Marked as Fraudulet
Triggers when KickoffLabs has scanned and marked a lead as fraudulent.
Lead Generated a Referral
Triggers when a lead gets another lead to signup for your campaign.
Lead Reward Level Reached
Triggers when a lead achieves a reward level in your campaign.
Lead Score Change
Triggers when a leads score changes.
Lead Tagged
Triggers when a Lead Tag has been applied to a lead.
Lead Marked as Verified
Triggers when a lead clicks on a verification link.
New Lead
Triggers when a new lead is added to your campaign.

Actions

Add or Update Lead
Adds a new lead to your campaign or updates values on a lead.
Approve Lead(s)
Mark a lead as valid regardless of any detected fraud flags that were identified.
Block Lead(s)
Blocks a lead from your contest by marking them as fraudulent.
Get Campaign Stats
This will return some of your campaign stats.
Delete Lead(s)
Delete's a lead from you campaign.
Action - Mark as Complete for Lead
Marks a lead as having completed an action from your campaign.
Add Points to Lead
Add or remove custom points to a lead for whatever reason you'd like.
Tag Lead
Tags a lead with a lead tag that's been defined on your campaign.
Tag Lead Parent
Adds a lead tag to the parent, if one exists, of the lead being called in this tag. Useful if you want to award points to a lead that, for example, referred another lead to make a purchase.
Remove Lead(s) From Leaderboard
Remove one or more leads from your contest/waitlist leaderboard ranking. The lead(s) are NOT DELETED and their points are not decreased. Instead, they are excluded from the contest score rankings so others can move up and win.
Verify Lead(s)
Mark a lead has having a verified email address. Works the same as if they had clicked the verify email link.
Add Lead(s) Back to Leaderboard
This will re-add your lead(s) to your contest leaderboard or waitlist ranking. Their position will be based on their contest_score again. This is now the same as adding a lead. This only changes if existing leads (you have removed) are added back to the leaderboard.
Find Lead by Email
Finds a specific lead by Email address or Social ID.
Find or Add a Lead
Finds a specific lead
