Interseller Integrations

Interseller helps you find contacts' email addresses, send them messages and push them into any CRM or ATS with one-click.

Triggers

Contact Replied
Triggers when a contact replies back to a sequence.
New Contact
Triggers when a new contact has been added to any of your campaigns.

Actions

Create Contact
Creates a new contact.
Update Contact
Updates a contact that already exists inside Interseller.
Mark Contact Replied
Indicate a contact as responded
Find Contact by Email
Look for a Interseller contact by their email address.
