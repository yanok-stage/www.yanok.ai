Habitify Integrations

Habitify is a habit tracker that helps you form habits that actually stick. It’s designed to motivate you every day and reward you with beautiful streaks.

New Log
Triggers when a new log is added to your habit.
New Mood
Triggers when a new mood is created.
New Note
Triggers when a new note is added to your habit.
New Status
Triggers when a new status is updated.

Actions

Add Log
Add logs to your habits
Add Mood
Creates a new Mood
Add Note
Adds a note to your habit
Update Status
Update status of a habit
