Dataclay QUE Integrations

QUE is the optimal API that leverages the full capabilities of Templater for Adobe After Effects to produce dynamic video content.

Triggers

New Autograf
Triggers when a new Autograf is added to QUE.
New Campaign
Triggers when a new QUE Campaign is created.
New Job
Triggers when a new Job is inserted into a QUE Campaign.
New Satellite
Triggers when a new Satellite is added to your QUE account.
Updated Job
Triggers when a QUE Job is updated.

Actions

Insert Job
Inserts a Job into a QUE Campaign.
Update Job
Update an existing QUE Job.
Filter Jobs (Advanced)
Filters the Jobs in a QUE Campaign (this is an advanced action).
