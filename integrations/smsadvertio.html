smsadvert.io Integrations

Connect your phone(s) and use it as a gateway to send SMS messages or send them directly over the smsadvert.io network.

Triggers

Actions

Add Contact
Add contact to one of your smsadvert.io Groups. You can use them later to create SMS campaigns.
Send SMS
Sends SMS through your smsadvert.io account. You can choose to send the message both through our network and through your connected phone(s).
