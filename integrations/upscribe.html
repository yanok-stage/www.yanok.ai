Upscribe Integrations

Upscribe enables you to create embedable newsletter signup forms for Medium, SquareSpace, WordPress, and other awesome platforms.

Triggers

Email Sent
Triggers when an email is sent.
New Contact
Triggers when a contact is created in your account (via form submission or import).
New Form Submission
Triggers when a form is submitted.
New Signup (Deprecating)
Triggers when a contact is created in your account (via form submission or import).

Actions

Create Contact
Creates a new contact in your Upscribe account.
Send Email
Sends an email to all your contacts.
Send Single Email
Sends an email to one contact.
Tag Contact
Adds a tag to a contact.
