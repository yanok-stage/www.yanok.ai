Insightly Integrations

Insightly is a CRM and project management app that helps small to medium businesses manage contacts, track relationships, monitor sales pipeline, organize projects and more. It keeps all your critical business info on all your devices, so your company sells more and satisfies customers better.

Triggers

Deleted Record
Triggers when a record is deleted.
New Record
Triggers when a new record is created.
Updated Field on Record
Triggers when a field of your choosing (i.e. email address, status) is updated on any record of a specified Insightly object (ie. Contact, Lead, Opportunity, etc.).
Updated Record
Triggers when a record is updated.
