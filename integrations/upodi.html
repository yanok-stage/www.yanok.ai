Upodi Integrations

Upodi is a subscription and billing management platform.

Triggers

New Contact
Triggers when a new contact is created in Upodi.
New Customer
Triggers when a new customer is created in Upodi.
New Invoice
Triggers when a new invoice is created by Upodi or manually.
New Subscription
Triggers when a new subscription is created.
Updated Contact
Triggers when a contact is updated in Upodi.
Updated Customer
Triggers when a customer is updated in Upodi.
Updated Invoice
Triggers when an invoice is updated in Upodi.
Updated Subscription
Triggers when a subscription is updated in Upodi.

Actions

Activate Subscription
Activate a subscription to start invoicing automatically.
Add Optional Charge to Subscription
Add an optional charge to a draft or active subscription
Book Draft Invoice or Credit Note
Book a draft invoice or credit note in Upodi
Cancel Subscription
Cancels an active subscription
Create Contact for a Customer
Create a new contact for a customer
Create Customer
Create a new customer in Upodi
Create Payment Method for Customer
Create a Payment Method for a Customer
Create Subscription
Create a new subscription for a customer
Credit Invoice
Credit an invoice in Upodi. Creates a draft credit note which can then be booked
Switch Product Plan for a Subscription
Perform an immediate switch plan for the subscription from one Product Plan to another. If you would rather want a switch in the future on a set date check SwitchToPlanID on Subscription Update. If the change incurs an invoice the invoice will be returned here.
Update Contact
Updates a contact
Update Customer
Updates a customer
Update Subscription
Update a subscription for a customer
Create a Change to a Charge
When the subscription is created all charges will have the default quantities and prices. If you want to change them make sure to update them before you activate the subscription or update them along the way if what the customer purchases changes. If the change incurs an invoice the invoice will be returned here.
Find Charges on Subscription
Finds the charges on the subscription. Charges are products available on the subscription. Set quantity on the charges to subscribe the customer to the product.
Find Charges for a Product Plan
Find charges on a product plan
Find Contact
Find a contact for a customer optionally specifying a type
Find Customer
Finds a customer using accountnumber or ID
Find Invoice
Find a specific invoice and its lines
Find Subscription
Find a specific subscription
