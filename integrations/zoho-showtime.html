Zoho ShowTime Integrations

Zoho ShowTime is a web-conferencing and an online virtual training tool that lets trainers engage with their attendees to drive self-improvement through feedback, and showcase their delivery skills on a secure cloud platform. It is available on the web and as a mobile app.

Triggers

New Trainee Joins
Triggers when a new trainee joins a training session.
New Trainee Registers
Triggers when a new trainee registers for a training session.
New Session Is Created
Triggers when a new training session is created.

Actions

Invite People
This action sends an invitation email to your prospects for your training sessions.
Enroll a Registrant
This action registers a new trainee for a session.
Create a New Session
This action creates a new training session
