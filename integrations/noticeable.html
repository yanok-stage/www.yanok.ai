Noticeable Integrations

Noticeable is an all-in-one solution to update your users effectively. Use it to engage your customers or your team with regular updates, get powerful feedback, and measure satisfaction.

Triggers

Email Subscription Updated
Triggers when an email subscription is updated.
New Publication Comment
Triggers when a new comment is sent for a publication.
Publication Created or Updated
Triggers when a publication is created or updated (and the "Forward to Zapier integrations" option is enabled on a per-publication basis in the Noticeable dashboard).
Publication Deleted
Triggers when a publication is deleted.
New Email Subscription
Triggers when a new email subscription is made on the specified project.
Email Subscription Deleted
Triggers when an email subscription is deleted from a project.

Actions

Update Email Subscription
Updates an email subscription.
Create Publication
Creates a new publication.
Create Email Subscription
Creates an email subscription.
Delete Email Subscription
Deletes an email subscription.
Search Email Subscription
Search for an email subscription by email address.
Email Subscription
Finds or creates a specific subscriber-find.
