Jasmin Integrations

Jasmin is a Cloud Management Software by PRIMAVERA BSS that allows you to track your business any time and anywhere. Jasmin allows you to manage sales, payments, stocks, receipts, orders, etc., offering you KPIs and tips for your business.

Triggers

New Customer
Triggers when a new customer is created.
New Contact
Triggers when a new contact is created.
New Invoice
Triggers when a new invoice is created.
New Memo
Triggers when a new memo is created.
New Sales Item
Triggers when a new Sale Item is created.
New Sales Order
Triggers when a new order is created.
New Delivery
Triggers when a new shipping delivery is created.

Actions

Create/Update Sales Item
Creates a new sales item or updates an existing sales item.
Create/Update Customer
Creates a new customer ou updates an existing customer.
Create/Update Contact
Creates a new Contact.
Create Invoice
Creates a new invoice.
Create Sales Order (Legacy)
Creates a new sales order.
Create Sales Order
Creates a new sales order.
Create Delivery
Creates a new shipping delivery.
