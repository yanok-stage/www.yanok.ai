Transloadit Integrations

Transloadit is a file encoding service that helps you handle file uploads, resize, crop and watermark images, make GIFs, transcode your videos, extract thumbnails, generate audio waveforms, and so much more. In short, Transloadit is a Swiss Army Knife for your files.

Triggers

New Assembly
Triggers when a new Assembly is created.
Assembly Result
Triggers when an Assembly with at least one result is finished.
Finished Image Face Detection
Triggers when an image face detection is complete.
Finished Assembly
Triggers when a new Assembly is finished.
Finished iPad Video Optimization
Triggers when an iPad video optimization is complete.
Finished WebM Conversion
Triggers when a video is completely converted to WebM.

Actions

Create Assembly
Creates a new Assembly from a Template.
Detect Faces
Detects the faces in an image.
Optimize Video
Optimizes a video for iPad.
Convert Video to WebM
Converts a video to WebM.
